Who I am

I am a Senior at Christopher Newport University pursuing a degree in Information Science with a minor in Business Administration. My academic success has earned me membership in the Computing Honor Society, Upsilon Pi Epsilon. I have 4 years of work experience in Business and Government Technology projects and my skill set encompasses web design, data modeling, java programming, graphic arts, and client relations. My passion is making people’s lives easier through the use of technology and my goal is to find a position doing meaningful work in a team driven environment.

What I Do Best

Web Design

I have 4 years of Web Design experience making front-end designs for businesses and governments. My primary experience is in customizing Wordpress themes and creating websites with Bootstrap 3.

Information System Design

As an Information Science major and I have taken numerous Systems Design courses. My projects have included modeling a basic library information system, a database system for particle accelerators and a drink recommendation system.

Java Programming

My concentration in Computer Science allowed me to learn Java. In my academic experience with Java, I have taken courses up to Data File Structures. My work experience with Java is teaching kids programming fundamentals through Minecraft.

Graphic Design

I have been creating graphics for over 5 years. My programs of choice are Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. My experiences with graphic design are designing packaging, creating custom images for websites, and creating logos for organizations.

Playing Music

Playing music has played a major role in most of my life. I play flute, piccolo, Irish tin whistle, and fife. I am currently a member of CNU's Marching Captains and CNU's University Band. My true musical passion is in Irish Music. I am also a member of the Band Service Fraternity Kappa Kappa Psi.

Chinese Tea

My other passion lies in high-quality Chinese Tea. I find great pleasure in the process of finding and preparing one's perfect cup of tea. I also truly believe that “Sharing Tea With a Fascinating Stranger is One of Life’s True Delights.” -Uncle Iroh
